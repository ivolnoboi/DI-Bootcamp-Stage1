Alvaro Soler

is a Spanish-German singer

1991

He was born in Sant Cugat del Vallès to a German father and to a Spanish-Belgian mother.

2001

He moved with his parents to Japan.

2010

Returning to Barcelona, he established the musical band Urban Lights alongside his brother and some friends.

2013

The band appeared on the Spanish music casting contest ¡Tú sí que vales! reaching the Final 8.

2014

Soler decided to drop the Urban Lights band to go solo establishing himself as a musical artist based in Germany.

2015

Based in Berlin, he released his single "El mismo sol" co-written by Soler, Yasmin Gabriel, Ali Zuckowski, Simon Triebel and John Speaks and produced by Triebel.

2015

Soler released his debut solo album Eterno Agosto on Universal Music.

2016

Soler was chosen as a judge on the tenth series of Italian talent show X Factor.

2017

He collaborated with Morat, performing in the song "Yo contigo, tú conmigo" for the film Despicable Me 3.

2021

Soler was the voice of Camilo Madrigal in the Italian and German dubbed versions of the Disney film Encanto.
